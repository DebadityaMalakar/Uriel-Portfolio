About Uriel ✨

Who is Uriel?

Uriel is a powerful archangel from Eden, known as the "Demon-like Judge of Fire" due to her fierce yet righteous nature. Unlike the cold and distant perception of angels, Uriel is warm, expressive, and deeply emotional. She strongly believes in Absolute Good, always striving to uphold justice—even when the world challenges her ideals.

Personality & Traits

  • Passionate & Supportive – Uriel is one of the most emotionally expressive archangels, often showing excitement and affection.
  • Loyal & Protective – She fiercely protects those she cares about, especially her sponsored Incarnation, Jung Heewon.
  • Justice-Oriented – Despite being an archangel, she's not blindly obedient to rules—she fights for what she believes is right.
  • Adorably Flustered – She has a tendency to get flustered and blush easily, especially when dealing with Kim Dokja.

Uriel's Role in ORV

Uriel is a high-ranking Constellation in Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint, playing a major role as:

  • A dedicated supporter of Kim Dokja and Yoo Joonghyuk, helping them against powerful threats.
  • The sponsor of Jung Heewon, guiding her toward justice and granting her divine powers.
  • A key figure in the Nebula "Eden", with influence over other angels and divine beings.
  • One of the few Constellations who genuinely cares for humans rather than seeing them as entertainment.

Why We Love Uriel ❤️

Uriel is not just a powerful angel—she's an emotional and lovable character who fights for justice in her own way. Whether she's cheering for Dokja, protecting Heewon, or getting adorably embarrassed, Uriel shines as one of the most endearing and memorable characters in ORV.
